Australian Mental Health Facts

  • 1/7 Australians will experience depression at some point in their lifetime.
  • 1/4 Australians will experience anxiety at some point in their lifetime.
  • Over 1/16 Australians are currently struggling with depression.
  • 1/7 Australians are currently experiencing anxiety.
  • Only half of Australians who are or have struggled with their mental health have sought professional help.
  • Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islanders are twice as likely to die from suicide.
  • Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islanders are three times more likely to be psychologically distressed.
  • 19.6% of LGBTIQA+ Australians currently experience psychological distress.
  • 1/5 LGBTIQA+ Australians currently experience depression - this is three times that of heterosexual rates.
  • 1/3 LGBTIQA+ Australians experienced some form of anxiety condition - this is double that of heterosexual rates.
