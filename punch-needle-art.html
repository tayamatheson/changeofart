My Personal Experience

Before I saw this punch needle art kit at Kmart, I didn’t know what punch needle art was, and I thought it was going to be incredibly difficult. I was worried about how this would affect my anxiety, because usually I need something simple but consuming to help with my anxiety. I was experiencing the overwhelming kind of anxiety, so that is quite a difficult mood to reverse without sleeping for myself.

I loved using the punch needle kit. I found it to be extremely calming. I think having something set out for me – the design, the colours, the layout – made it a lot more calming. I did not have the pressure to figure out everything by myself. Someone had already thought it through for me. I felt a substantial increase in happiness when I was completing this craft. The more I did, the more I wanted to do it. I was slowly, but surely, becoming obsessed with the sensation of punching through the fabric and watching the yarn create something beautiful.

This craft is a fantastic way for people to not only ease anxiety and stress, but to ease anger. Like how some suggest smashing plates to ease anger, I feel like using a needle to punch yarn through the fabric has a similar effect but less damaging. Instead of destroying something to ease anger, you are creating something that will not only stabilise your emotions but provide something to be proud of in the end. Anything that can make my anxiety easier to manage is something I want to keep doing. It doesn’t have to completely calm me; I just want things that make my life slightly easier than it was before I try them.
